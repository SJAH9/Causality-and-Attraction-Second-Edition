## Overview

The book presents a paradigm of nested steady-state dynamic equilibria that unifies physics, biology, economics, and human systems through three foundational laws:

- The Law of Nested Causal Enclosure  
- The Law of Nested Exchange  
- The Law of Nested Infinities

## Repository Structure

The manuscript is split into 8 files for easier editing:

- `1of8.tex` – Front matter + Foreword + Prologue
- `2of8.tex` – Chapters 2–3
- `3of8.tex` – Chapters 4–5
- `4of8.tex` – Chapters 6–8 + Epilogue
- `5of8.tex` – Appendices A–E
- `6of8.tex` – Appendices F–I
- `7of8.tex` – Appendices J–M
- `8of8.tex` – Appendices N–Q (including the final Vision appendix)

To compile the full book, concatenate the 8 files in order or use a master file that inputs them sequentially.

3. The final PDF will be approximately 55–60 pages.
